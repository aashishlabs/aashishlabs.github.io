Centralized PPI & Payments Reporting System

Creating a centralized system of records for PPI, AEPS, and BBPS transactions while automating regulatory reporting at YES Bank.

10M–15MTransactions managed monthly
40+Corporate prepaid clients
45+AEPS, BBPS & FASTag partners

The Context

At YES Bank, I managed digital banking products and partnerships across prepaid cards, wallets, AEPS, BBPS, and FASTag. The growing transaction and partner footprint created a need for a dependable, centralized view of operational and regulatory records.

The Challenge

Records were distributed across products and operational processes, making compliance reporting dependent on manual consolidation. At a scale of 10M–15M transactions per month, the approach increased effort and limited timely visibility across clients and partner activity.

My Role

As Product Manager for Digital Banking, I designed and delivered a centralized system of records covering PPI, AEPS, and BBPS transactions. I aligned operational, compliance, business, and technology requirements to automate RBI reporting and reduce manual overhead.

Reporting Flow

PPI / WalletsAEPS / BBPSTransaction RecordsValidation & ControlsCompliance Reporting

This simplified portfolio view excludes confidential client data, reporting logic, controls, and internal bank architecture.

Key Product Work

  • Mapped records across PPI, AEPS, and BBPS product flows.
  • Defined a centralized operational and compliance reporting view.
  • Aligned requirements across business, operations, compliance, and technology teams.
  • Automated RBI compliance reporting to replace manual consolidation.
  • Improved visibility across corporate clients, partners, and transaction activity.

Impact

  • Managed a platform footprint processing 10M–15M transactions per month.
  • Supported 40+ corporate clients across prepaid cards and wallets.
  • Supported 45+ partners across AEPS, BBPS, and FASTag.
  • Eliminated manual overhead from regulatory reporting workflows.

Key Learning

At payments scale, compliance reporting must be designed as a product capability. Reliable source records, clear ownership, and automated controls are essential for both regulatory confidence and operational visibility.
