The Context

At L&T Finance, I worked across digital transformation for rural lending products, including Farm and WRF. The opportunity was to improve lending decisions and reduce turnaround time by bringing structured data and automated decisioning into the existing loan journey.

The Challenge

Credit decisioning required close alignment between product, risk, data science, engineering, and the existing LOS/LMS ecosystem. The solution needed to translate underwriting requirements into dependable data inputs and decision logic without disrupting the wider lending journey.

My Role

I contributed to the product workstream from the CDO's office, helping define data inputs, decision logic, product requirements, and integration with the existing lending stack. I coordinated across data science, engineering, risk, and business stakeholders as the capability moved toward production.

Key Product Work

  • Translated rural lending needs into structured product and data requirements.
  • Helped define decision inputs and logic with risk and data science teams.
  • Aligned the decisioning capability with the existing LOS/LMS journey.
  • Supported cross-functional delivery across business and technology teams.
  • Applied Agile transformation practices to improve delivery speed.

Impact

  • Reduced loan disbursal turnaround time by 40%.
  • Accelerated product delivery by 50% through Agile transformation.
  • Supported digital transformation across two rural lending products.

Key Learning

AI credit decisioning succeeds when product, risk, data, and lending operations share a clear definition of every input, rule, exception, and downstream outcome.
